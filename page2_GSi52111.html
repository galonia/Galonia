Mysore, Karnatka

Mysore is one of the most popular cities in south. Its known for its exotic sandalwood and silk cloth. It has many tourist ttractions such as the mysore palace,Chamundeshwari temple, Mysore Zoo, Shuka Vana and lush green gardens. The rich heritage of ancient india is preserved here very beautifully by the government. There are many authentic resturants to have the basic and aroma rich south indian food. Thier is a dosa named after this city - MYSORE dosa rich in spices.

Kozhikode, Kerala

It is situated in Kerela. Its known as the hub of spice trading. The beaches here are popular spots to witness sunsets and sunrise. Other famous places to vist are mananchira square, kadalundi bird sanctuary, sarovaram biopark, blueC event hub , Thusharagiri waterfall to name the few.

Mumbai is famous across the globe for its non stop running life. The city never sleeps. The vibes of this city are soothing and resfreshing. All famous personlities and industrialist resides in bombay. Mumbaikars and people from other parts come to vist thier house from outside and celebrate thier b-days as a festival over thier. Sunsets are always pleasent to watch and sunset at JUHU beach, mumbai is cherry on the cake. Mumbai is also known for its spicy and tangy food such as bombay bhel, sev puri, pav-bhaji and so many other dishes. Some of the famous tourist spots are taj hotel, gateway of india, colaba market, haji ali and sea link.
